Project Description

Welcome to Pet Breed Classifier, where artificial intelligence meets your furry friends! Our application uses deep learning to identify dog and cat breeds from your uploaded images with impressive accuracy.


Simply upload a photo of any cat or dog, and our homemade ConvNeXt-Tiny model—trained on the comprehensive Oxford-IIIT Pet Dataset—will analyze the image and tell you not only whether it's a cat or dog, but also identify the specific breed with a confidence score.


Whether you're curious about your own pet's heritage, trying to identify a friend's adorable companion, or just want to browse through our gallery of previously classified pets, Pet Breed Classifier offers a fun, educational experience for animal lovers of all kinds.
